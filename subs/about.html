Our Story

Childfree Foundation Uganda which is located in Buzirandulu Village, Lwengo district Uganda was founded in 2013 as a community-based organization by an ambitious and gold hearted Walakira Fuluje Edwine.

The influx of the HIV/AIDS epidemic in the 1990s left many Ugandan toddlers orphans and helpless as most of them lost their parents to the epidemic. Walakira together with a team of competent world heroes being victims as well, were prompted to lay a helping hand to these orphaned children through the Childfree Foundation. Their focal point was on availing basic life necessities and education to the vulnerable children. They started with only 7 children in the year 2013 but currently have a sum of 73 children.

Farming was our major source of finances from the word go but as time went on, we managed to obtain other gold hearted members and sponsors the likes Auntie Nantege Racheal the acting chairperson, Uncle Michael the acting secretary and later Uncle Tonny Shunnarah from USA our trustworthy treasurer.

In an effort to build trust among our stake holders and always project genuinity and authenticity in every operation we do, we went an extra mile to register our organisation with the Government of Uganda. We were in position to obtain the Certificate of Registration & Permit for operation. See for yourself..

To Childfree Foundation Uganda the sky is the limit.......

Our Mission

Our mission is to change the world for tomorrow's children by providing them with the basic life requirements needed to thrive..

Our Values

Integrity.

We function with honesty, trustworthy and care in whatever act of charity we engage in as we value the interests of all our stake holders.

Compassion.

None of the children we help signed up to be in such a situation that’s why we sympathize and we as well concerned about the suffering of the orphans.

Accountability.

All our stakeholders have a right to know the details and progress of our operations and each dime spent without limitations.

Transparency.

We disclose all our operations to our sponsors and donors we gat nothing to hide, no back room deals.

Our Vision

We exist to witness a world where every child no matter what walk of life they come from, has access to improved basic life needs.
